Dynamic structure

The dynamic structure means that the entire form is assembled like lego cubes - from buttons. These buttons are customized at your discretion.

To customize the buttons, you have keys that you insert into the text of your button from a script or plugin. This allows you to achieve maximum freedom, and most importantly, an unlimited number of variations of your forms.

Advanced technologies

GrimUI is filled with the most incredible technologies, some of which do not exist in any UI! These technologies are related not only to design, but also to optimization.

• Soft padding centralization

• Text buffer fill-based paddings

• Advanced vertical processing

And so much more!

Fullstack & Ready-to-go UI

GrimUI has already been prepared and configured for release to your server. You just need to assemble your forms! GrimUI has both a long form and a custom form, which allows you to fully immerse yourself in the Ore-UI style.

GrimUI will work on any device, adapting to the user's screen and its proportions.

If you have difficulty creating your own forms, which is quite simple, then you can always take ready-made templates.
